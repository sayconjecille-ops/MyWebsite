Level of Satisfaction in the Services Offered by Honda Bay Boat Owners Service Cooperative (HBBOSCO)

Tourism significantly contributes to the Philippines' socio-economic growth, but challenges remain in achieving sustainable and inclusive development. The Honda Bay Boat Owners Service Cooperative (HBBOSCO) plays a vital role in tourism at Honda Bay by providing boat services to visitors. This study aimed to assess tourist satisfaction with HBBOSCO‚Äôs services and identify factors influencing their experience. A total of 138 local and 135 foreign tourists who used the boat services were surveyed using close-ended questionnaires. Findings show that local tourists are satisfied with customer service and very satisfied with boat services. Foreign tourists are very satisfied with both customer and boat services provided by HBBOSCO.
